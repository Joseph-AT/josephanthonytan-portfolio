What is RFM?

RFM Analysis is a powerful marketing tool that segments customers based on their transaction history.

By understanding these segments, businesses can tailor their referral and loyalty programs effectively, ensuring they resonate with the right audience.

Automation Workflow

What is KNIME?

KNIME is a free, open-source data analytics platform that lets you build automation workflows visually (without heavy coding) to clean data, automate tasks, run machine learning models, and create end-to-end data solutions.

The analysis reveals clear opportunities to boost revenue by prioritizing high value customer segments, reactivating churned users, strengthening product and geographic strategies, and optimizing campaigns around peak sales periods.

Processing Details:

Monetary was calculated using Qty * price formula, Recency was processed using Date&Time Difference, Frequency was processed by GroupBy, and Binning was processed for assigning quantiles and labeling.

Market Basket Analysis (MBA)

What is MBA?

Market Basket Analysis is a data mining technique used to identify products that customers frequently buy together, helping businesses discover buying patterns and create effective cross-sell, upsell, and product placement strategies.

Market Basket Analysis (MBA)

Uncover MBA using KNIME

Association Rule Mining is a data mining technique used to discover relationships, patterns, or co occurrences between items in large datasets, commonly used in market basket analysis. Identifies items that frequently occur together and quantifies the strength of their relationships using metrics like Support, Confidence, and Lift.

MBA Overview Go to Tableau Public KNIME (MBA)

Summary of the Business Insights:

Sales are driven by core categories like poultry, soda, and cereals, but overall revenue has declined since 2019 with clear mid year and weekday seasonal patterns leveraging strong cross sell behaviors and routine replenishment trends from Market Basket Analysis, the store should boost core-category promotions, introduce day specific offers, optimize product placement, and create bundled deals to increase basket size, stabilize dips, and improve inventory planning.
